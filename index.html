FAQ

Good questions, straight answers

How much does window cleaning cost?

Most Paradise Valley homes run between $250 and $650 depending on size, glass count, and whether we're doing interior + exterior. Hard water restoration is quoted per pane. We'll text you an exact number before any work begins — no surprises, ever.

Can hard water stains actually be removed?

Usually, yes. Mineral deposits from sprinklers, misters, and rain runoff etch into glass over time. Caught early enough, professional restoration removes them completely. Severely etched glass we'll assess honestly — if restoration won't work, we'll tell you before charging you.

How often should windows be cleaned in Arizona?

For Paradise Valley homes we recommend exterior cleaning every 3–4 months (dust and monsoon season are relentless) and interior twice a year. Most of our clients move to a quarterly plan after their first cleaning — it keeps glass protected and costs less per visit.

Do I need to be home?

Only for interior work. For exterior-only jobs, many clients simply leave the side gate unlocked — we text before arrival, send before/after photos, and lock up when we're done.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

Tell us within 48 hours and we come back and make it right at no charge — no fine print, no debate. Our entire business in Paradise Valley is built on referrals, so an unhappy customer costs us far more than a return visit.
