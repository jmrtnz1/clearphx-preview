An Atelier, Not a Service

One craftsman. Every pane treated as a finished surface — never a chore.

Most window companies send a rotating crew that has never seen your home. ClearPHX is the opposite by design: a single, accountable owner whose reputation lives on every reflection in the house. The work is quiet, exacting, and documented — the way fine things are looked after.
